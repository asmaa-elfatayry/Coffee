Cappuccino

Place a cup of milk in the microwave, uncovered, for one minute,
or until the milk is hot and small bubbles form. Whisk hard forwards and backwards until the volume of foam grows then put espresso and the hot milk. into the cup, Foam is poured on top

Cortado

Grind your coffee of choice. Then Brew around for 20 seconds
and reserve. get one shot of espresso. and heat the milk until
it is just foamy. finally, add the milk over the espresso
gently, keep it layered.

Affogato

first add two scoops of ice cream.Secondly, add chocolate shavings or other toppings if you desire to. finally pour two shots of hot espresso or a filter brewed coffee over the ice cream.

Macchiato

Macchiato is made with espresso, requires 20g of coffee beans. Grind the beans. make the espresso shot. then Steam the milk at
60 C. pour the steamed milk onto the espresso. scoop a dollop of
foam on top. You can add sugar, garnish the top with cinnamon

Turkish

Bring water and sugar to a boil. Remove from heat and add
coffee and cardamom. Return to heat and allow to come to a
boil. Remove from heat when coffee foams. Finally, return
to heat, allowing to foam and remove from heat.
